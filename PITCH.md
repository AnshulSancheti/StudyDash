# StudyDash

### The all-in-one school management platform built for Indian schools.

---

## The Problem

Indian schools today manage their daily operations through a patchwork of disconnected tools — WhatsApp groups for parent communication, paper registers for attendance, Excel sheets for fees, and phone calls to track school buses. This creates:

- **For Parents:** No single place to check attendance, fees, homework, or bus location. They rely on WhatsApp forwards and phone calls to stay informed about their child's school day.
- **For Teachers:** Hours spent on manual attendance registers, paper homework tracking, and fielding individual parent messages — time that should go to teaching.
- **For School Administrators:** Fee collection is chaotic (cash, cheques, UPI screenshots), record-keeping is fragmented across spreadsheets, and there's zero visibility into operational spending.
- **For Students:** No centralized access to their timetable, notes, assignments, or academic progress.

Schools that try existing solutions find them either too expensive, too complex, or built for Western school systems that don't understand Indian board structures (CBSE, ICSE, State Boards), regional languages, or the way Indian schools actually operate.

---

## The Solution

**StudyDash** replaces the chaos with one unified platform — a mobile app for teachers, parents, and students, and a web dashboard for school administration.

Everything a school needs to run its daily operations, in one place, designed specifically for how Indian schools work.

---

## What StudyDash Does (MVP)

### Instant Attendance Visibility
Teachers mark attendance on their phone in under 60 seconds. Parents get an instant push notification if their child is absent — no more waiting until evening to find out.

### Homework & Notes — No More Lost Papers
Teachers post assignments and upload class notes directly to the app. Students see everything organized by subject with due dates. Parents can check what homework was assigned without asking their child.

### Live Bus Tracking
Parents see their child's school bus on a live map with ETA to their stop. They get a notification when the bus is approaching — no more standing at the stop wondering where the bus is.

### Digital Fee Collection
Parents pay fees online (UPI, cards, net banking) directly through the app. Schools see real-time payment status, can record cash payments, and generate collection reports instantly. No more chasing defaulters manually — the system sends automated reminders.

### Parent-School Communication
A proper channel for announcements and complaints — not a WhatsApp group. Teachers send class updates, parents raise queries that get tracked as tickets until resolved. Admins broadcast school-wide announcements.

### Timetable at a Glance
Students and parents always know what's happening today — which subjects, which periods, which teachers. Teachers see their schedule across all classes.

### Leave Management
Parents apply for leave through the app. Teachers approve or reject with one tap. Approved leaves automatically update attendance records — no manual coordination needed.

### Expense Tracking for Management
School admins log all operational expenses (salaries, maintenance, utilities, events) with receipt uploads. Monthly summaries and category breakdowns give instant visibility into where money is going.

### Smart Notifications
Every important event triggers a push notification to the right person — absence alerts to parents, new homework to students, fee reminders to defaulters, bus arrival to waiting parents. No one misses what matters.

## What Makes StudyDash Different

### 1. Built for India, Not Adapted for India
StudyDash is designed from the ground up for Indian schools — CBSE/ICSE/State Board structures, fee installment patterns that match how Indian parents pay, Hindi and regional language support on the roadmap, and UPI-first payment integration.

### 2. Pixel Trace — AI-Powered Photo Discovery (Coming Post-MVP)
This is our moat. Schools host dozens of events each year — sports days, cultural programs, annual days — and take thousands of photos. Parents never find their child's photos in that flood.

**Pixel Trace** uses face recognition to automatically match event photos to students. Parents open the app and see only their child's photos — instantly, without scrolling through hundreds of images. No other school management platform offers this.

Pixel Trace also lays the foundation for **AI Attendance** — a classroom photo that automatically marks the roll call — eliminating manual attendance entirely.

### 3. Simple by Design
Most school management software tries to do everything and ends up overwhelming users. StudyDash focuses on the 10 things that matter most and makes each one dead simple. A teacher can mark attendance for 45 students in under a minute. A parent can pay fees in 3 taps. An admin can onboard an entire school in an afternoon.

### 4. Affordable for Every School
Our pricing will be accessible to budget-conscious Indian schools — not just premium international schools. The platform is designed to scale efficiently, keeping per-school costs low.
